About Us

At AK BIM, we specialize in providing high-quality electrical BIM services tailored for the construction and energy industries. Our team of skilled engineers and modelers is dedicated to delivering accurate, efficient, and intelligent designs that drive successful projects.

With a strong background in electrical design and coordination, we specialize in creating accurate and detailed BIM models tailored to your project needs. Our expertise ensures seamless integration with other disciplines, reducing errors, saving time, and improving overall project efficiency.

Why Us

With years of experience in electrical BIM, we provide accurate, detailed, and reliable models that support every stage of your project. Our focus on precision and coordination reduces risks, improves collaboration, and ensures smooth project delivery.

What We Do

Electrical BIM Modeling

Accurate 3D models with detailed electrical system layouts for efficient planning and execution.

Coordination & Clash Detection

Ensure conflict-free integration with other disciplines using industry-standard coordination tools.

Electrical Documentation
